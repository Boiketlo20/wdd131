Bag Bliss - Website Plan

Site Plan

Bag Bliss

This name represents an online store specializing in trendy, functional bags for travel, work, and everyday use

Site Puprpose

This is where people can discover stylish and functional handbags and accessories. Bag Bliss will sell high-quality bags (backpacks, totes, travel bags) and provide styling tips for customers.

Scenarios

  • What types of handbags do you offer (e.g., totes, crossbody, backpacks, clutches)?
  • Are these bags spacious enough for everyday essentials like laptops, water bottles, or makeup?
  • Are your handbags budget-friendly or more of a luxury investment?

Color Schema:

Primary Color: #F9DEC9- used for headers and buttons.

Secondary Color #333333 - used for text and borders.

Accent Color 1 #FFB6C1 - call-to-action buttons, icons, or important text highlights.

Accent Color 2 #F56FA1 - over effects, dividers, or subtle background details.

Typography

Headings: Poppins

Body Text: Roboto Condensed
